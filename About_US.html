Our Shining Fame Story

Let us show you Our Shining Fame Story as we go beyond our first decade. Moving forward from our simple beginnings, we invite you to join us and help write the next chapter of Shining Fame!

Where We Started

Shining Fame is a computer retail business founded on the values of convenience, accessibility, and excellent customer service. Believe it or not, this leader in local technology retail had pretty humble beginnings – when five friends who were passionate about tech hunted down secondhand PCs in the Tipid PC online group to resell at decent rates.

Where We’re Going

Today, as we serve you in-store and online, our dedication to growth also extends to each and every customer. We strive to provide solutions, reliable services, and affordable but quality technologies that will enhance the lives of our customers and community.

As we move to greater heights, giving you the best customer experience is still our number one goal.
